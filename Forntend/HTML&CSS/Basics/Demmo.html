The word "aspiring" is an adjective that describes someone who has strong ambitions, goals, or desires to achieve something, often in the context of their career or personal development. An aspiring person is someone who is working towards a particular goal or striving to become something or someone in the future. It implies a sense of determination and a willingness to put in the effort required to achieve one's aspirations. For example, an aspiring musician is someone who is working towards becoming a professional musician, and an aspiring writer is someone who is actively working on their writing with the goal of becoming a published author.
