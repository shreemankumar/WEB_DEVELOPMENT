Attendance Manager

An attendance manager, often referred to as an attendance management system, is a software or tool designed to help organizations or institutions track and manage the attendance of their employees, students, or other members. It is a valuable tool for maintaining attendance records, monitoring attendance trends, and ensuring compliance with attendance policies.
